Tale as old as ... like two years ago

Haley and Sean met on the internet. Their first date was at a wine bar on California Avenue in Palo Alto, with an exotic name — Calaaaave. Or was it just named after the street? They liked each other enough for a second date. They kissed. Or tried to and bonked noses. Sean thought it was cute. Haley didn't but stuck around. They kept seeing each other. They said I love you, unshowered after backpacking while downing oysters and cava. Sean's weekend getaways at Haley's became everyday life. They bought rings and put them in kayaks. And then on each others fingers. And then into oyster shucking gloves. Now they're getting married. We'll see about the rest.
