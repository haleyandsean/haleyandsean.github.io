Travel Information

Discounts Passing the savings on to youuuu! We are pleased to offer a rental car discount code for Hertz. Use 2187703 for 20% off your booking.

Where do I fly in? San Luis Obispo (SBP) is the closest airport to Paso (~30 min drive/Uber-able), and likely the best option if you're not planning on renting a car. While it's a little smaller of an airport, most places have a connecting flight option. If you are renting a car and don't mind driving a few hours, any of the airports in the Bay Area (likely SFO or SJC) are good options, as is Los Angeles (LAX). The drive from either of these is ~3.5 hours. Let us know if you need any assistance or recommendations. There are some connecting bus/train options from the Bay/LA, but these will probably add time to your overall trip.

Where do I stay? We suspect vacation rentals will be a better option for most people than hotels, and recommend you do that. There are several good options:

  • Paso Robles Vacation Rentals: https://pasoroblesvacationrentals.com/
  • Airbnb
  • VRBO
  • Local winery guest houses

If you're staying in Paso beyond the wedding (even if just for a day or two), we recommend staying within walking distance of the main town square for access to restaurants, tasting rooms, etc. Use Downtown City Park as your reference point on a map.

When should I RSVP by? April 28th, 2025, but as soon as possible will help us with planning. If you need more time, please reach out.

Am I allowed to bring my children? We've included them on your invitation, please indicate in your RSVP if they're coming or not. If you don't see a spot for them and expect one, let us know. While we're happy to have them in attendance, please note that we will not be providing childcare or child-specific entertainment (no wine for kids), so you will be responsible for them.

Am I allowed to bring a plus one? We've set up our RSVP system to automatically grab the number of invitations associated with your name. If you don't seem to have enough slots, feel free to reach out — our web developer* isn't paid enough, so might've left some bugs in there.

What time should I arrive? You're welcome to come to the venue starting at 3:00pm for a welcome drink. We plan to start the ceremony promptly at 3:30pm. Cocktail hour and reception will follow, starting from around 4:00pm.

What time should I leave? We need to stop playing amplified music by 10:00pm because of a county-wide noise curfew. Everyone needs to be off the property by 11:00pm. We will likely try to coordinate an after-party, stay tuned for details. For our guests wishing to depart earlier, we should be done with dinner and starting the dance party somewhere in the 7:00pm - 8:00pm area.

Transportation, what's the deal with that? See above for details on flights and accommodation. The venue is a ~10 min drive from downtown Paso. If you plan to drink, we strongly encourage taking Uber/Lyft, and there is plenty of availability of Ubers and Lyfts both from the venue and in Paso Robles itself. If you aren't drinking or have a designated driver, there is parking available at the venue, and overnight parking is ok if you need to Uber home. Because of the rideshare availabilty, we will not be offering a shuttle service, but let us know if you have transportation concerns.

At night it may cool down a bit, so an extra layer might be needed for our guests who run a little cold. Our wedding colors are bright and citrusy, so summery, sunny, colorful outfits are encouraged, but not required. In terms of "official" categories, we're targeting the regime of "festive" and "semi-formal".
